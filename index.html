about TETI






TETI is a start up in IT and electronics education. We based on Tengaran and Our Workshop now at Jarimatika Coworking Space placed on Jalan Margosari PR 4 Salatiga, Jawa Tengah Indonesia.

We always try to alternate the way of learning so every body can learn coding, or electronics or networking. Our dream is everybody in everywhere can learn and undestand either coding or electronics or networking on their best way.

We provide you courses and kits because of our mission as facilitator, we want you are able to learn and create your own, whatever (related to coding, electronics or networking) you need.
